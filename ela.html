Ela

Ela was known as the chic woman of her town—not because of expensive clothes, but because of her confidence and grace. She walked with calm strength, spoke with kindness, and carried herself with quiet elegance. Wherever she went, people noticed her positive energy. Ela loved books, simple fashion, and meaningful conversations. She believed that true beauty came from intelligence and self-respect.
