3 Neamat, Ela, and Zitu were known as the “three pagol” among their friends. One sunny morning, they decided to visit the National Parliament of Bangladesh. Neamat acted like a serious guide, explaining history with confidence—most of it slightly wrong. Ela kept correcting him, reading facts from the information board and laughing at his mistakes. Zitu, the real pagol, tried to copy the guards’ serious walk and almost fell, making everyone burst into laughter..

Ekdin shokal-e 3 Pagol ber holo bina kono plan-e. Karo mathay chinta nai—“kothay jabo, ki korbo”—just mon bolse, ber hotei hobe! 🌿

Prothom Pagol chilo shobcheye confident. Camera ta haate niye bollo, “Aaj amra memory banabo!” 📸

Ditiyo Pagol chilo silent type, kintu action-e sharp. Ekta thumbs up diye bujhiye dilo— “Life chill, no tension!” 👍
